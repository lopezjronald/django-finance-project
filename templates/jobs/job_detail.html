About Us

Scalp Trade is a proprietary trading firm that was formed over 10 years ago. During this time, Scalp has expanded its core business of options trading to include virtually every asset class of exchange-traded products. One of our goals is to create more efficient markets through technology, innovation, and providing needed liquidity, while creating tighter spreads.
The Scalp team is built on the foundation of collaboration, diversity and innovation; we work hard to foster a culture that our team truly enjoys. As Scalp continues to grow, we are always looking for those who are technology oriented, embrace challenges and enjoy problem solving. While we are committed to critical thinking, we want more than a degree. We really focus on the intangibles; we embrace individuals with grit, self-motivation, and those with the desire and experience to tackle the most difficult of obstacles
